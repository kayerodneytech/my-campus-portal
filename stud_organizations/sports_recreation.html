Sports & Recreation

Stay active, relieve stress, and build camaraderie by joining one of MyCamp Portal's sports teams or recreational clubs. We cater to various interests and skill levels.

Football (Soccer) Club

Join the MyCamp Rovers and compete in inter-college leagues. Develop your skills, teamwork, and fitness on the pitch.

Record/History: Defending inter-college champions (2023 season), finalists in the MyCamp Cup for 3 consecutive years.

Requirements: Passion for football, good sportsmanship, basic fitness.

Training Times: Weekly, Tuesdays & Thursdays 2:00 PM - 5:00 PM. Scrimmage games all day Saturday.

Basketball Team

Dribble, shoot, and score with the MyCamp Hoopers! Perfect your game and enjoy competitive matches in a supportive environment.

Record/History: Top 3 finish in the Bulawayo College League (2023), strong contender for upcoming season.

Requirements: Love for basketball, commitment to training, willingness to learn.

Training Times: Weekly, Mondays & Wednesdays 2:00 PM - 5:00 PM. Friendly matches all day Saturday.

Netball Club

Join our dynamic Netball team, the MyCamp Queens! Improve your agility, passing, and shooting in a fun and competitive setting.

Record/History: Undefeated in the internal MyCamp Challenge (2024), developing talent for regional tournaments.

Requirements: Enthusiasm for netball, team spirit, basic fitness.

Training Times: Weekly, Tuesdays & Fridays 2:00 PM - 5:00 PM. Practice matches all day Saturday.

Chess Club

Sharpen your mind and strategize your way to victory! The MyCamp Chess Club welcomes players of all levels.

Record/History: Regular participants in provincial tournaments, numerous individual student victories.

Requirements: Interest in chess, logical thinking, patience.

Meeting Times: Weekly, Wednesdays 2:00 PM - 5:00 PM. Casual play all day Saturday in the common room.

Recreational Activities

  • Games Room: Open daily, 8:00 AM - 8:00 PM (Pool, Table Tennis, Board Games)
  • Hiking Club: Monthly excursions to scenic spots around Bulawayo and Matopos Hills.
  • Debate & Public Speaking Society: Weekly meetings to hone your communication skills.

For registration and more information on any sport or club, please contact the SRC Sports & Recreation Officer.
