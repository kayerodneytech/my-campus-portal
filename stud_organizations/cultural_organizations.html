Cultural & Faith-Based Organizations

MyCamp Portal celebrates diversity and encourages students to explore their cultural and spiritual interests. Our various organizations provide platforms for fellowship, cultural exchange, and personal growth.

Seventh-day Adventist Students Association (SASA)

A fellowship for Adventist students focused on spiritual growth, health principles, and community outreach.

A supportive community for ZCC students to practice their faith, share traditions, and engage in campus activities.
