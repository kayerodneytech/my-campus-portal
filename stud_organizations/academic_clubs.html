Academic Clubs

Enhance your learning experience and connect with like-minded students by joining our academic clubs. These clubs offer workshops, study groups, guest lectures, and competitions to deepen your knowledge and skills.

IT Innovators Club

For students passionate about programming, web development, cybersecurity, and emerging tech. Host hackathons and coding challenges.

Focus: Software Development, Networking, AI

Contact: it.innovators@mycamp.ac.zw

Engineering & Robotics Society

Bringing together mechanical, electrical, and civil engineering students to work on practical projects and robotics challenges.

Focus: Practical Engineering, Robotics, Design

Contact: eng.robotics@mycamp.ac.zw

Business & Entrepreneurship Forum

A hub for aspiring entrepreneurs and business leaders. Discuss market trends, pitch ideas, and network with industry professionals.

Focus: Business Strategy, Startups, Marketing

Contact: biz.forum@mycamp.ac.zw

Culinary Arts Guild

For students in Food Preparation, Professional Cookery, and Culinary Arts. Explore new recipes, catering opportunities, and food styling.

Focus: Cooking Techniques, Food Safety, Hospitality

Contact: culinary.guild@mycamp.ac.zw

Beauty & Cosmetology Collective

Connecting students from Hairdressing, Beauty Therapy, and Cosmetology. Share techniques, organize beauty expos, and learn from industry experts.

Focus: Hair Care, Skin Care, Makeup Artistry, Salon Management

Contact: beauty.collective@mycamp.ac.zw
