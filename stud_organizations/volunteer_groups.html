Volunteer Groups

Make a positive impact in the community and gain valuable experience by joining one of our volunteer groups. Your time and effort can truly make a difference!

Campus Clean-Up Crew

Dedicated to maintaining a clean and green campus environment. Regular clean-up drives and recycling initiatives.

Activities: Litter picking, garden maintenance, awareness campaigns.

Contact: Environment Committee, SRC Welfare Officer

Community Outreach Team

Engages with local communities through various initiatives like supporting orphanages, assisting the elderly, or participating in educational drives.

Activities: Visits, fundraising, mentorship programs.

Contact: SRC Community Liaison

Academic Support Mentors

Senior students offering peer-to-peer tutoring and study support to junior students in challenging subjects.

Activities: Tutoring sessions, study tips workshops.

Contact: SRC Academic Affairs Officer

Interested in volunteering? Look out for recruitment drives at the start of each semester or contact the relevant SRC officer.
